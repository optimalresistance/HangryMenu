Ingredients
  • 100 g dark chocolate (70%)
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 2 tbsp milk or cream
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • Pinch of salt
  • Chopped almonds for topping
Recipe
  1. Melt chocolate with milk and almond butter until smooth; cool slightly.
  2. Whisk in egg yolks and honey.
  3. Beat egg whites with a pinch of salt until soft peaks form; fold gently into chocolate mixture.
  4. Chill for 2 hours; top with chopped almonds before serving.
DessertServes 3
