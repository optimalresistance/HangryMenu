Berry Oat Crumble Bars ▾

Ingredients
  • 1½ cups rolled oats
  • ¾ cup whole-wheat flour
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup melted butter or coconut oil
  • 2 cups mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • Pinch of salt
Recipe
  1. Preheat oven to 180 °C / 350 °F.
  2. Mix oats, flour, sugar, salt, and butter until crumbly.
  3. Press ⅔ of mixture into lined baking pan.
  4. Toss berries with syrup and cornstarch; spread over base.
  5. Sprinkle remaining oat mix; bake 25–30 min until golden.
DessertServes 3
