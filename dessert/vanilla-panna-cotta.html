Ingredients
  • 2 cups cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 packet gelatin
Recipe
  1. Bloom gelatin in milk 5 minutes.
  2. Heat cream & sugar to steamy; remove from heat.
  3. Whisk in vanilla and bloomed gelatin until dissolved.
  4. Pour into cups; chill 4+ hours.
