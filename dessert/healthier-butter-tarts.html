Healthier Butter Tarts ▾

Ingredients
  • 6 small tart shells (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 egg
  • ⅓ cup maple syrup
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Pinch of salt
  • ¼ cup raisins or chopped dates
  • 2 tbsp chopped walnuts (optional)
Recipe
  1. Preheat oven to 190 °C / 375 °F.
  2. Whisk egg, syrup, sugar, butter, vanilla, and salt together.
  3. Add raisins and nuts; fill tart shells two-thirds full.
  4. Bake 15–18 min until golden and just set.
  5. Cool before serving.
DessertServes 3
