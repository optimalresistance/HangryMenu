Oatmeal Raisin Cookies (Lower Sugar) ▾

Ingredients
  • 1½ cups rolled oats
  • ¾ cup whole-wheat flour
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup butter or oil
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup raisins
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Pinch of salt
Recipe
  1. Preheat oven to 180 °C / 350 °F.
  2. Cream butter and sugar; add egg and vanilla.
  3. Mix dry ingredients separately; combine with wet mixture.
  4. Fold in raisins; scoop onto tray.
  5. Bake 10–12 min until edges are set.
DessertServes 3
