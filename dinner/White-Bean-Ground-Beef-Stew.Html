White Bean & Ground Beef Stew

  • 300 g lean ground beef
  • 1.5 cups cooked white beans (or canned, rinsed)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups hot water or broth
  1. Heat olive oil in a pot and sauté onion until golden.
  2. Add garlic and ground beef. Cook until browned.
  3. Stir in cumin, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, then the beans and broth.
  5. Simmer 25–30 minutes until thick and aromatic.
DinnerServes 3
