Macaroni Béchamel (Egyptian-style) ▾

Ingredients
  • 200 g macaroni
  • 250 g ground beef
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp butter, 1 tbsp flour, 1 cup milk (for béchamel)
  • ¼ cup grated cheese (optional)
  • Salt, pepper, nutmeg
Recipe
  1. Cook macaroni until al dente.
  2. Sauté onion and beef; add tomato paste and seasonings.
  3. Make béchamel by whisking butter, flour, milk until thick; season with salt and nutmeg.
  4. Layer half the macaroni → meat → remaining macaroni → béchamel → cheese.
  5. Bake at 190 °C / 375 °F for 20–25 min until golden.
DinnerServes 3
