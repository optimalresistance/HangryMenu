White Bean & Ground Beef Stew ▾

Ingredients
  • 300 g lean ground beef
  • 1.5 cups cooked white beans (or canned, rinsed)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 cups water or broth
Recipe
  1. Sauté onions in olive oil until golden.
  2. Add garlic and ground beef; cook until browned.
  3. Add cumin, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stir in tomatoes, beans, and broth.
  5. Simmer 25–30 minutes until thick and aromatic.
Dinner Serves 3
