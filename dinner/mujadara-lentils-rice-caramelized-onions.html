Mujadara (Lentils + Rice + Caramelized Onions) ▾

Ingredients
  • ¾ cup brown lentils
  • ¾ cup long-grain rice
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin, ½ tsp allspice
  • Salt & pepper
Recipe
  1. Cook lentils 10 min in salted water; add rice and 2 cups water, cook 15 min until tender.
  2. Meanwhile, fry onions in olive oil slowly 15–20 min until deep golden.
  3. Mix half the onions into lentil-rice; season with spices.
  4. Top with remaining caramelized onions and serve warm or at room temp.
DinnerServes 3
