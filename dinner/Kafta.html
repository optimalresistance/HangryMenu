Kafta (Grilled or Baked) ▾

Ingredients
  • 400 g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 small onion, grated
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 tsp allspice
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Salt & pepper
Recipe
  1. Mix all ingredients well.
  2. Form patties or skewers.
  3. Cook at 400°F (200°C) for 15–18 min, or grill until browned.
Dinner Serves 3
