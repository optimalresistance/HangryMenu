Garlic Liver & Onions

  • 300 g beef or chicken liver, sliced thin
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. In a skillet, heat oil and sauté onions until soft.
  2. Add garlic, stir 30 seconds, then push to the side.
  3. Add liver slices, season, and cook 2–3 min per side.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice over top and serve immediately.
DinnerServes 3
