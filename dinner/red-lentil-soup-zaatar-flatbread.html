Red Lentil Soup + Za’atar Flatbread ▾

Ingredients
  • ¾ cup red lentils
  • 1 small carrot + ½ onion chopped
  • 1 clove garlic + 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp olive oil + 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt, pepper, lemon juice
  • 3 small flatbreads + 1 tbsp za’atar + olive oil (for topping)
Recipe
  1. Sauté onion, carrot, and garlic in olive oil 2–3 min.
  2. Add lentils, broth, and cumin; simmer 15 min until lentils soften.
  3. Blend slightly for smooth texture; season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  4. Brush flatbreads with olive oil + za’atar; toast 5 min at 200 °C / 400 °F.
  5. Serve soup hot with crispy flatbread on the side.
DinnerServes 3
