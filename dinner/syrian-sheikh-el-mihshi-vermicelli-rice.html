Syrian Sheikh el Mihshi + Vermicelli Rice ▾

Ingredients
  • 2 medium eggplants
  • 200 g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup yogurt (optional)
  • 1 cup vermicelli rice
  • Oil, salt, pepper, pinch of cinnamon
Recipe
  1. Slice eggplants lengthwise, salt lightly, and pan-fry or roast until golden.
  2. Sauté onion and beef; season with salt, pepper, cinnamon; add 2 tbsp tomato sauce.
  3. Stuff eggplant halves with meat mixture and arrange in baking dish.
  4. Cover with remaining tomato sauce and bake 20 min at 180 °C / 350 °F.
  5. Serve with cooked vermicelli rice and optional yogurt on the side.
DinnerServes 3
