Sheikh El Mehshi — Stuffed Zucchini in Warm Yogurt Sauce ▾

Ingredients
For the Stuffed Zucchini
  • 8–10 small zucchinis (koosa)
  • 250 g ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts (optional but authentic)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp allspice
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Salt & pepper to taste
For the Yogurt Sauce
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 egg (to stabilize yogurt)
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp dried mint
  • Salt to taste
For the Side
  • 1.5–2 cups vermicelli rice
Recipe
  1. Wash zucchinis, cut off stems, and hollow them using a corer.
  2. Sauté onion in olive oil, then add ground beef, pine nuts, allspice, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook until browned, then fill each zucchini with the beef mixture.
  4. Lightly pan-fry the stuffed zucchinis until golden (optional but adds flavor).
  5. In a pot whisk yogurt, cornstarch, and egg until smooth. Heat on low while stirring continuously.
  6. Add garlic, dried mint, and salt. Continue cooking until it gently simmers.
  7. Add the stuffed zucchinis to the yogurt sauce and simmer 20–25 minutes.
  8. Serve warm over vermicelli rice.
Dinner Serves 3
