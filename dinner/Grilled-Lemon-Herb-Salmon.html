Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon + Quinoa Salad ▾

Ingredients
  • 3 salmon fillets (120 g each)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice + zest
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ tsp paprika, salt, pepper
  • ¾ cup quinoa
  • 1 small cucumber, diced
  • ½ red pepper, diced
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 tbsp olive oil + 1 tsp vinegar (for dressing)
Recipe
  1. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper; marinate salmon 10–15 min.
  2. Cook quinoa according to package; toss with chopped vegetables, parsley, and dressing.
  3. Grill or pan-sear salmon 3–4 min per side until flaky.
  4. Serve salmon over quinoa salad; drizzle extra lemon juice on top.
DinnerServes 3
