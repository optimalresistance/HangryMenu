Chinese Beef Stir-Fry

  • 300 g beef strips
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell pepper, broccoli, carrots)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 tsp ginger powder (or fresh grated)
  1. Coat beef with soy sauce and cornstarch; marinate 10 min.
  2. Heat oil in a wok, sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add beef, stir-fry until browned.
  4. Add vegetables and oyster sauce; cook 5–7 min until tender-crisp.
DinnerServes 3
