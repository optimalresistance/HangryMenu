Chinese Beef Stir-Fry ▾

Ingredients
  • 300 g beef strips
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, peppers, carrot)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
Recipe
  1. Toss beef with soy sauce + cornstarch; marinate 10 min.
  2. Sauté garlic and ginger in hot oil.
  3. Add beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add vegetables + oyster sauce; cook 5–7 min.
Dinner Serves 3
