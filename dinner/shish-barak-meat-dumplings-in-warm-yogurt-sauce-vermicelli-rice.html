Shish Barak (Meat Dumplings in Yogurt Sauce) + Rice ▾

Ingredients
  • 18 small dumplings (filled with seasoned ground beef/onion)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ tsp dried mint
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 cup cooked vermicelli rice
Recipe
  1. Bake or pan-sear dumplings 5 min until lightly golden.
  2. Whisk yogurt with cornstarch + ½ cup water; heat gently while stirring.
  3. Add garlic, mint, salt; simmer 5 min until thickened.
  4. Drop dumplings into sauce 3–4 min until tender.
  5. Serve over vermicelli rice.
DinnerServes 3
