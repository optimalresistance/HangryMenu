Spaghetti + Meatballs ▾

Ingredients
  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 300 g ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400 mL) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil + 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt & pepper, olive oil
Recipe
  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente; drain.
  2. Mix beef, egg, breadcrumbs, Parmesan, and half the garlic; season and form 9–10 meatballs.
  3. Brown meatballs in olive oil, remove.
  4. In same pan, sauté remaining garlic; add crushed tomatoes, paste, herbs, and simmer 10 min.
  5. Return meatballs, simmer 10 min until cooked through. Serve over spaghetti.
DinnerServes 3
