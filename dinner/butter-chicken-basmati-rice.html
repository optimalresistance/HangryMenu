Butter Chicken + Basmati Rice ▾

Ingredients
  • 400 g chicken breast or thigh, cubed
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • ½ cup tomato purée
  • ½ cup cream (or coconut milk)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic + 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp garam masala, ½ tsp cumin, ½ tsp chili powder
  • Salt & pepper
Recipe
  1. Cook rice:
    1. Rinse rice: Wash basmati 2–3 times until water runs almost clear.
    2. Add to pot: Combine rinsed rice, water, salt, and optional butter/oil.
    3. Bring to boil: Heat on high until bubbling.
    4. Simmer: Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook for 12 minutes (do not open lid).
    5. Rest: Turn off heat and let the rice sit covered for 10 minutes.
    6. Fluff: Use a fork to fluff gently before serving.
  2. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger in butter until golden.
  3. Add spices and chicken; cook 5–7 min.
  4. Pour in tomato purée; simmer 10 min.
  5. Stir in cream, simmer 5 min until thick. Serve with rice.
DinnerServes 3
