Butter Chicken + Basmati Rice ▾

Ingredients
  • 400 g chicken breast or thigh, cubed
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • ½ cup tomato purée
  • ½ cup cream (or coconut milk)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic + 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp garam masala, ½ tsp cumin, ½ tsp chili powder
  • Salt & pepper
Recipe
  1. Cook rice according to package directions.
  2. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger in butter until golden.
  3. Add spices and chicken; cook 5–7 min.
  4. Pour in tomato purée; simmer 10 min.
  5. Stir in cream, simmer 5 min until thick. Serve with rice.
DinnerServes 3
