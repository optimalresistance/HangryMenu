Chili + Rice ▾

Ingredients
  • 300 g ground beef or turkey
  • 1 can kidney beans (400 g)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (400 g)
  • ½ onion + 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp chili powder, ½ tsp paprika, ½ tsp cumin
  • 1 cup rice (cooked separately)
  • Oil, salt, pepper
Recipe
  1. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add ground meat and spices; cook until browned.
  3. Stir in beans and tomatoes; simmer 15 min until thick.
  4. Adjust seasoning and serve over hot rice.
DinnerServes 3
