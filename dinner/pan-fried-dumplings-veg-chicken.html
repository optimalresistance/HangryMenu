Pan-Fried Dumplings (Veg + Chicken) ▾

Ingredients
  • 15 dumpling wrappers
  • 150 g ground chicken
  • ½ cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp soy sauce + ½ tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Oil + 2 tbsp water (for steaming)
Recipe
  1. Mix filling: chicken, cabbage, onion, soy, sesame, ginger.
  2. Place 1 tsp filling in wrapper; fold and seal edges.
  3. Heat oil in pan; arrange dumplings flat-side down until golden.
  4. Add 2 tbsp water, cover, steam 2–3 min.
  5. Uncover and crisp bottoms; serve with soy dipping sauce.
DinnerServes 3
