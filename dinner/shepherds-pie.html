Shepherd’s Pie ▾

Ingredients
  • 300 g ground beef or lamb
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • ½ cup peas
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp butter + ¼ cup milk
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce, salt & pepper
Recipe
  1. Cook onion and carrot until soft; add beef and brown.
  2. Add tomato paste, Worcestershire, and peas; simmer 5 min.
  3. Spread into baking dish.
  4. Top with mashed potatoes (mixed with butter + milk).
  5. Bake 200 °C / 400 °F for 15 min until golden.
DinnerServes 3
