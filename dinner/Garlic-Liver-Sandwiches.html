Garlic Liver Sandwiches (Pressed & Crispy) ▾

Ingredients
  • 300 g beef or chicken liver, thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt & pepper
  • 4 small rolls or pita
  • 2 tbsp tahini or garlic sauce (optional)
  • Butter or oil for pressing
Recipe
  1. Sauté onions in oil until soft.
  2. Add garlic; cook 30 sec until fragrant.
  3. Add liver, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook 2–3 min per side.
  4. Squeeze lemon on top.
  5. Assemble sandwiches with optional tahini/garlic sauce.
  6. Brush outside with butter and heat-press until crispy.
Dinner Serves 3
