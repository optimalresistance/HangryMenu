Garlic Liver Sandwiches (Crispy Pressed)

  • 300 g beef or chicken liver, thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 small sandwich rolls or pita breads
  • 2 tbsp tahini or garlic sauce (optional)
  • Butter or oil for pressing
  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet and sauté onions until soft and golden.
  2. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add liver slices, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook 2–3 minutes per side until browned but still tender.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice on top and remove from heat.
  5. Spread a bit of tahini or garlic sauce inside each roll, fill with the liver mixture and onions.
  6. Brush the outside of the sandwich with butter or oil and heat-press in a sandwich press or skillet with a weight until golden and crispy on both sides.
DinnerServes 3
