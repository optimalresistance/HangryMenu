Week 2 — Shopping List ▾

Proteins
  • Ground beef or lamb ~450 g (Kafta + zucchini stuffing)
  • Chickpeas 1 can (or 1.5 cups cooked)
  • Pine nuts 2–3 tbsp (optional but authentic for Sheikh El Mehshi)
Grains / Pasta
  • Lasagna sheets (250–300 g)
  • Vermicelli 1/2 cup (for rice)
  • Long-grain rice ~2 cups
  • Pita bread or naan (for chickpea soup)
Vegetables & Herbs
  • Onions 4–5
  • Garlic 1 bulb
  • Spinach (fresh 3–4 cups or frozen equivalent)
  • Mushrooms 2 cups sliced
  • Zucchini 8–10 small (Sheikh El Mehshi)
  • Parsley small bunch (Kafta)
  • Lemon 2
Canned / Dairy / Pantry
  • Marinara or crushed tomatoes (700 mL total)
  • Tomato paste 1 small can
  • Ricotta or cottage cheese 1 small tub (for lasagna)
  • Mozzarella 1 block or 2 cups shredded
  • Coconut milk 1 can (for chickpea soup)
  • Plain yogurt 2 cups (Sheikh El Mehshi sauce)
  • Cornstarch 1 tbsp (for yogurt sauce)
  • Eggs (1 for yogurt stabilization)
  • Olive oil
Spices & Seasonings
  • Allspice, cinnamon (Sheikh El Mehshi + Kafta)
  • Cumin, coriander (chickpea soup)
  • Basil, oregano (lasagna)
  • Dried mint 1 tbsp (Sheikh El Mehshi)
  • Salt & pepper
