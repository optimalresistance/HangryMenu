Week 2 — Shopping List ▾

Proteins
  • Ground beef or lamb ~450 g (Kafta + zucchini stuffing)
  • Chickpeas 1 can (or ~1.5 cups cooked)
  • Pine nuts 2–3 tbsp (optional, for Sheikh El Mehshi)
Grains / Pasta
  • Lasagna sheets 250–300 g
  • Vermicelli 1/2 cup (for rice)
  • Long-grain rice ~2 cups
  • Pita bread or naan (for soup side)
Vegetables & Herbs
  • Onions 4–5
  • Garlic 1 bulb
  • Spinach 3–4 cups (fresh) or frozen equivalent
  • Mushrooms ~2 cups sliced
  • Zucchini 8–10 small (Sheikh El Mehshi)
  • Parsley small bunch (Kafta)
  • Lemon 2
Canned / Dairy / Pantry
  • Marinara or crushed tomatoes ~700 mL
  • Tomato paste 1 small can
  • Ricotta or cottage cheese 1 tub (lasagna)
  • Mozzarella 2–3 cups shredded
  • Plain yogurt 2 cups (Sheikh El Mehshi sauce)
  • Cornstarch 1–2 tbsp
  • Eggs ~6 (Sheikh El Mehshi + panna cotta + brownies)
  • Coconut milk 1 can (soup)
  • Milk or cream 500–700 mL (panna cotta + brownies)
  • Butter 1 block (baking + cooking)
  • Sugar (granulated + optionally brown)
  • Cocoa powder (brownies)
  • Dark chocolate or chocolate chips (brownies)
Dessert & Baking
  • Gelatin (powdered or sheets) for panna cotta
  • Vanilla extract (panna cotta + brownies)
  • All-purpose flour ~3–4 cups
  • Baking powder (if your brownie recipe uses it)
Spices & Seasonings
  • Allspice, cinnamon (Sheikh El Mehshi + Kafta)
  • Cumin, coriander (chickpea soup)
  • Basil, oregano (lasagna)
  • Salt, pepper
  • Dried mint 1 tbsp (Sheikh El Mehshi)
