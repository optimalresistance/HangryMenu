Week 4 — Shopping List ▾

Proteins
  • Chicken (minced or finely chopped) ~300 g (dumplings)
  • Beef or chicken liver ~300 g (sandwiches)
  • Ground beef ~400 g (shepherd's pie)
Grains / Dumpling Wrappers
  • Long-grain rice ~2 cups (mujadara)
  • Brown or green lentils ~1 cup
  • Dumpling / potsticker wrappers (~20–24)
Vegetables & Herbs
  • Onions 6–7 large (mujadara + liver sandwiches + shepherd's pie)
  • Garlic 1 bulb
  • Carrots 2 (shepherd's pie)
  • Celery 2 stalks (optional for shepherd's pie)
  • Peas 1 cup (shepherd's pie)
  • Potatoes 4–5 large (shepherd's pie topping)
  • Green onion (optional – dumplings)
  • Parsley small bunch (optional)
  • Lemon 1 (liver sandwiches)
Canned / Dairy / Pantry
  • Butter (for mashed potatoes + dumplings)
  • Milk or cream (for mashed potatoes)
  • Soy sauce (dumplings)
  • Vinegar (dumpling dipping sauce)
  • Olive oil
  • Bread (for crispy pressed liver sandwiches)
Spices & Seasonings
  • Cumin (mujadara + liver sandwiches)
  • Paprika (liver sandwiches + shepherd's pie)
  • Cinnamon or 7-spice (optional for shepherd's pie depth)
  • Salt, pepper
