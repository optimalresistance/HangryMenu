Week 4 — Shopping List ▾

Proteins
  • Chicken (minced or finely chopped) ~300 g (dumplings)
  • Beef or chicken liver ~300 g (sandwiches)
  • Ground beef ~400 g (shepherd’s pie)
Grains / Dumpling Wrappers
  • Long-grain rice ~2 cups (mujadara)
  • Brown or green lentils ~1 cup
  • Dumpling/potsticker wrappers ~20–24
  • Rolled oats ~2–3 cups (crumble + bars)
  • Flour ~3–4 cups (scones + thickening)
Vegetables & Herbs
  • Onions 6–7 large (mujadara + liver + shepherd’s pie)
  • Garlic 1 bulb
  • Carrots 2–3 (shepherd’s pie)
  • Celery 2 stalks (optional, shepherd’s pie)
  • Peas 1–1.5 cups (shepherd’s pie)
  • Potatoes 4–5 large (shepherd’s pie mash)
  • Green onion (optional – dumplings)
  • Parsley small bunch
  • Lemon 1 (liver sandwiches)
  • Mixed berries (fresh or frozen) ~2–3 cups (crumble bars)
Canned / Dairy / Pantry
  • Butter 1–2 blocks (mash + dumplings + desserts)
  • Milk or cream (for mashed potatoes + scones)
  • Soy sauce (dumplings)
  • Vinegar (dumpling dipping sauce)
  • Olive oil
  • Maple syrup ~1/2–1 cup (scones + bars)
  • Pecans ~1 cup (maple pecan scones)
  • Sugar (for desserts, light use)
Dessert & Baking
  • Baking powder (scones)
  • Eggs ~3–4 (scones + binding)
  • Vanilla extract (optional, bars)
  • Salt (for dough + scones)
Spices & Seasonings
  • Cumin (mujadara + liver sandwiches)
  • Paprika (liver + shepherd’s pie)
  • Salt, pepper
