Week 3 — Shopping List ▾

Proteins
  • Ground beef ~350–400 g (béchamel pasta + stew)
  • Chicken drumsticks or thighs ~500–600 g (fried chicken)
Grains / Pasta / Legumes
  • Macaroni pasta 400 g
  • Long-grain rice ~2 cups (maqluba)
  • Brown or green lentils (if using in maqluba, optional)
  • White beans 1–2 cans (or ~2 cups cooked)
  • Rolled oats ~2–3 cups (cookies + crumble topping feel)
Vegetables & Herbs
  • Onions 5–6
  • Garlic 1 bulb
  • Eggplant 1 large (maqluba)
  • Potatoes 4–5 medium (maqluba + fries)
  • Cauliflower 1 small (maqluba)
  • Carrots 2 (stew or shepherd-like flavors)
  • Tomatoes 2–3
  • Lemon 1–2
Canned / Dairy / Pantry
  • Crushed tomatoes 1 can (stew)
  • Milk 3–4 cups (béchamel + desserts)
  • Butter 1–2 blocks (béchamel + frying + desserts)
  • Flour ~3–4 cups (béchamel + baking)
  • Bread crumbs or coating mix (fried chicken)
  • Oil for frying
  • Pitted dates ~1–1.5 cups (sticky toffee pudding)
  • Baking soda (for dates)
  • Sugar (reduced for cookies + pudding)
Dessert & Baking
  • Raisins 1 cup (oatmeal cookies)
  • Eggs ~4–5 (desserts + béchamel if used)
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking powder (cookies/pudding)
  • Cinnamon (cookies, optional)
Spices & Seasonings
  • Cumin, cinnamon (stew)
  • Allspice / 7-spice (maqluba)
  • Paprika, garlic powder, onion powder (fried chicken)
  • Nutmeg (optional, béchamel)
  • Salt, pepper
