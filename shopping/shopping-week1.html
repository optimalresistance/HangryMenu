Week 1 — Shopping List ▾

Proteins
  • Ground beef ~900 g (meatballs + shish barak filling + stir-fry)
  • Chicken (thighs/breast) ~400–500 g (butter chicken)
  • Plain yogurt ~2 cups (shish barak + optional marinade)
  • Eggs (2 for meatballs)
Grains / Pasta / Wrappers
  • Spaghetti 200 g
  • Basmati rice ~2 cups (butter chicken + vermicelli rice)
  • Fine vermicelli 1/2 cup
  • Dumpling/mini-wonton wrappers (for shish barak) ~20–24
  • Bread crumbs 1/4 cup
Vegetables & Herbs
  • Onions 5–6 large (all meals)
  • Garlic 1 bulb
  • Ginger small knob (butter chicken + stir-fry)
  • Parsley small bunch (meatballs + shish barak)
  • Bell peppers (2) (stir-fry)
  • Broccoli 1 small head (stir-fry)
  • Carrots (2) (stir-fry)
  • Lemon 2 (shish barak garnish + general)
Canned / Dairy / Pantry
  • Crushed tomatoes (400 mL) 1 can
  • Tomato paste 1 small can
  • Coconut milk or heavy cream 1 cup (butter chicken)
  • Soy sauce (stir-fry)
  • Oyster sauce (stir-fry)
  • Cornstarch (stir-fry)
  • Olive oil, butter
Spices & Seasonings
  • Garam masala, turmeric, cumin, chili powder (butter chicken)
  • Oregano, basil, dried mint (shish barak + spaghetti)
  • Salt, black pepper
  • Ginger powder (optional, for stir-fry)
