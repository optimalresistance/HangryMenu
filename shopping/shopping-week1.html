Week 1 — Shopping List ▾

Proteins
  • Ground beef ~850 g (meatballs + shish barak filling)
  • Chicken (thighs/breast) ~400–500 g (butter chicken)
  • Plain yogurt ~2 cups (shish barak + optional marinade)
  • Eggs 2
Grains / Pasta / Wrappers
  • Spaghetti 200 g
  • Basmati rice ~2 cups (butter chicken + vermicelli rice)
  • Fine vermicelli 1/2 cup
  • Dumpling/mini-wonton wrappers (for shish barak) ~20–24
  • Breadcrumbs 1/4 cup
Vegetables & Herbs
  • Onions 4–5 large
  • Garlic 1 bulb
  • Ginger small knob
  • Parsley small bunch
  • Lemon 2
Canned / Dairy / Pantry
  • Crushed tomatoes (400 mL) 1 can + tomato paste 1 small can
  • Heavy cream or coconut milk 1/2 cup
  • Parmesan (block or grated) small piece
  • Olive oil, butter
Spices & Seasonings
  • Garam masala, cumin, chili powder
  • Oregano, basil, dried mint
  • Salt, pepper
