Week 1 — Shopping List ▾

Proteins
  • Ground beef ~900 g (meatballs + shish barak + stir-fry)
  • Chicken (thighs/breast) ~400–500 g (butter chicken)
  • Plain yogurt ~2 cups (shish barak + optional marinade)
  • Eggs ~6 (meatballs + desserts)
Grains / Pasta / Wrappers
  • Spaghetti 200–300 g
  • Basmati rice ~2 cups (butter chicken + vermicelli rice)
  • Fine vermicelli 1/2 cup
  • Dumpling/mini-wonton wrappers ~20–24 (shish barak)
  • Breadcrumbs 1/4 cup (meatballs)
  • Rolled oats ~2 cups (cookies)
  • Tart shells (8–10 small) or pastry
Vegetables & Herbs
  • Onions 5–6 large
  • Garlic 1 bulb
  • Ginger small knob (butter chicken + stir-fry)
  • Parsley small bunch (meatballs + shish barak)
  • Bell peppers 2 (stir-fry)
  • Broccoli 1 small head (stir-fry)
  • Carrots 2 (stir-fry)
  • Lemon 2
Canned / Dairy / Pantry
  • Crushed tomatoes 1 can (spaghetti sauce or stew base)
  • Tomato paste 1 small can
  • Coconut milk or cream ~1 cup (butter chicken)
  • Butter 1 block (cooking + desserts)
  • Olive oil
  • Brown sugar or maple syrup (butter tarts + cookies)
  • Raisins or chopped nuts (butter tarts)
Dessert & Baking
  • Walnuts ~1 cup (maple walnut cookies)
  • All-purpose flour ~3–4 cups
  • Baking powder and/or baking soda
  • Vanilla extract (cookies + tarts)
  • Cinnamon (optional, cookies)
Spices & Seasonings
  • Garam masala, turmeric, cumin, chili powder (butter chicken)
  • Oregano, basil, dried mint (pasta + shish barak)
  • Soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch (stir-fry)
  • Salt, black pepper
