Maple-Pecan Scones ▾

Ingredients
  • 1½ cups whole-wheat flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ cup cold butter
  • ⅓ cup chopped pecans
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • ⅓ cup milk
  • ½ tsp vanilla
Recipe
  1. Preheat oven to 200 °C / 400 °F.
  2. Combine dry ingredients; cut in butter until crumbly.
  3. Add pecans, maple syrup, milk, and vanilla; mix into dough.
  4. Shape into circle, cut wedges, place on tray.
  5. Bake 12–15 min until golden; drizzle with extra maple syrup if desired.
DessertServes 3
