Raspberry “Bakewell” Oat Bars ▾

Ingredients
  • 1½ cups rolled oats
  • ½ cup ground almonds
  • ¾ cup whole-wheat flour
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup melted butter or coconut oil
  • 1 cup raspberry jam or crushed raspberries
  • ½ tsp vanilla
  • Pinch of salt
Recipe
  1. Preheat oven to 180 °C / 350 °F.
  2. Mix oats, almonds, flour, sugar, salt, butter, and vanilla until crumbly.
  3. Press half into pan, spread jam, then sprinkle remaining mixture on top.
  4. Bake 25–30 min until golden and fragrant.
  5. Cool before slicing into bars.
DessertServes 3
