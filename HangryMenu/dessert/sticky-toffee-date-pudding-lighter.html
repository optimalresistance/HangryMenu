Sticky Toffee Date Pudding (Lighter) ▾

Ingredients
  • ¾ cup chopped pitted dates
  • ½ cup boiling water + ½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup whole-wheat flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ cup maple syrup (for sauce)
  • 1 tbsp butter + 2 tbsp milk (for sauce)
Recipe
  1. Pour boiling water and baking soda over chopped dates; rest 10 min.
  2. Cream butter and sugar; whisk in egg.
  3. Add flour, baking powder, salt, and date mixture; stir to combine.
  4. Bake at 180 °C / 350 °F for 20–25 min.
  5. Simmer sauce ingredients; drizzle over warm pudding.
DessertServes 3
