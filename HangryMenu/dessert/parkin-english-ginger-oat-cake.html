Parkin (English Ginger Oat Cake) ▾

Ingredients
  • ¾ cup oats
  • ¾ cup whole-wheat flour
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup molasses or maple syrup
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ⅓ cup milk
  • Pinch of salt
Recipe
  1. Preheat oven to 170 °C / 340 °F.
  2. Melt butter, molasses, and sugar together.
  3. Mix dry ingredients; add wet mixture and milk until smooth.
  4. Pour into greased dish and bake 25–30 min.
  5. Cool completely before slicing.
DessertServes 3
