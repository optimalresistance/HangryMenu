Fudgy Skillet Brownies ▾

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • Pinch salt & vanilla
Recipe
  1. Heat oven to 350°F (175°C). Melt butter in skillet.
  2. Whisk in sugar, eggs, cocoa, flour, salt, vanilla.
  3. Bake 18–22 min until set at edges and glossy in center.
