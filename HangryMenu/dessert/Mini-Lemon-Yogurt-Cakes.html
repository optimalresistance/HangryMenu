Mini Lemon Yogurt Cakes ▾

Ingredients
  • ¾ cup whole-wheat flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • ⅓ cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or melted butter
  • ¼ cup sugar or honey
  • Zest + juice of 1 lemon
  • Pinch of salt
Recipe
  1. Preheat oven to 180 °C / 350 °F.
  2. Whisk wet ingredients (yogurt, egg, oil, lemon juice, zest, sweetener).
  3. Mix dry ingredients separately; fold together until smooth.
  4. Divide into 6 muffin cups and bake 15–18 min until golden.
  5. Cool slightly; drizzle with a bit of honey or powdered sugar if desired.
DessertServes 3
