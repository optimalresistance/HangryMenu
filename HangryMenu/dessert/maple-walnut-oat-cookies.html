Maple Walnut Oat Cookies ▾

Ingredients
  • 1½ cups rolled oats
  • ¾ cup whole-wheat flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ⅓ cup butter or olive oil
  • ½ cup maple syrup
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 tsp vanilla
Recipe
  1. Preheat oven to 180 °C / 350 °F.
  2. Whisk butter, maple syrup, egg, and vanilla together.
  3. Mix dry ingredients in another bowl; combine both.
  4. Fold in walnuts; scoop dough onto tray.
  5. Bake 10–12 min until golden around edges.
DessertServes 3
