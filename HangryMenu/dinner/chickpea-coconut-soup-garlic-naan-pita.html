Chickpea Coconut Soup + Garlic Naan ▾

Ingredients
  • 1 can chickpeas (400 g), drained
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 small onion + 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice, salt & pepper
  • 3 naan or pita breads, brushed with garlic butter
Recipe
  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  2. Add curry powder and chickpeas; cook 2 min.
  3. Pour in broth and coconut milk; simmer 10 min.
  4. Blend half for creaminess; season with lemon juice, salt, pepper.
  5. Warm naan, brush with garlic butter; serve alongside soup.
DinnerServes 3
