Veg Lasagna (spinach-mushroom, marinara + béchamel) ▾

Ingredients
  • 6 lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup spinach (chopped fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (marinara)
  • 1 tbsp butter + 1 tbsp flour + 1 cup milk (for béchamel)
  • Salt, pepper, nutmeg
Recipe
  1. Cook noodles until just tender.
  2. Sauté mushrooms and spinach in a pan until soft; season.
  3. Make béchamel by whisking butter, flour, and milk until smooth and thick; season with nutmeg and salt.
  4. Layer: marinara → noodles → spinach/mushroom → ricotta → béchamel. Repeat twice.
  5. Top with mozzarella and bake at 190 °C / 375 °F for 25 min until bubbly and golden.
DinnerServes 3
