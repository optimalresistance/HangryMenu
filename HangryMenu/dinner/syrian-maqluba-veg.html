Syrian Maqluba (Veg) ▾

Ingredients
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 eggplant, 1 potato, 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (for base)
  • 1 tsp allspice, ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
Recipe
  1. Fry or roast vegetable slices until lightly golden.
  2. Arrange tomato slices at bottom of pot, layer veggies, then rice and spices.
  3. Pour broth to cover rice by 1 cm; bring to boil, then cover and simmer 15–20 min.
  4. Rest 5 min, then flip onto plate carefully (“maqluba” = upside-down).
  5. Serve with yogurt or salad.
DinnerServes 3
