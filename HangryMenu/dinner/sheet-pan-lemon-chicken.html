Sheet-Pan Lemon Chicken & Veg ▾

Ingredients
  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 1 lemon (zest & juice)
  • 2 cups chopped veg (potato, carrot, onion)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper
Recipe
  1. Heat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Toss veg with half the oil, salt, pepper. Spread on tray.
  3. Rub chicken with remaining oil, lemon, oregano; season.
  4. Roast 25–35 min until chicken is 165°F/74°C and veg tender.
