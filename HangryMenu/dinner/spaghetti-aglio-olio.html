Ingredients
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • Pinch red pepper flakes
  • Salt & pepper
  • Parsley, chopped
  • Lemon (optional)
Recipe
  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. Warm oil, gently toast garlic and pepper flakes.
  3. Toss pasta with oil, add splashes of pasta water to coat.
  4. Season, finish with parsley and a squeeze of lemon.
