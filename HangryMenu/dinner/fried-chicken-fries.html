Fried Chicken + Fries ▾

Ingredients
  • 3 chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • ½ cup buttermilk (or milk + ½ tsp lemon)
  • ½ cup flour
  • ½ tsp paprika, ½ tsp garlic powder, salt, pepper
  • Oil for frying
  • 3 medium potatoes (for fries)
Recipe
  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk 15 min (minimum).
  2. Mix flour with spices; coat chicken pieces well.
  3. Heat oil and fry chicken 10–12 min per side until golden and cooked through.
  4. Cut potatoes into fries; fry or bake until crisp.
  5. Serve with ketchup or garlic mayo.
DinnerServes 3
